Pricing FAQ

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no hidden fees. Cancel your subscription at any time and you'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

What does the founding member price lock mean?

Founding members pay $29/month for Pro access permanently. Even as prices change, your rate stays locked for as long as your subscription is active. It never increases.

Can I switch plans?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. If you upgrade mid-cycle, you'll be credited for the remaining time on your current plan.

Is REMi financial advice?

No. REMi provides algorithmically generated market intelligence and confidence signals. It is not a financial advisor and does not constitute financial advice. All investment decisions are yours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards. Payment processing is handled securely through Stripe.
